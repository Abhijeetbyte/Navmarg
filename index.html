OUR WORK

What we've done for people

M.A.R.U

MARU - Magnetic Arsenic Removal Unit: A Transformative Story Navmarg Research and Innovation's MARU (Magnetic Arsenic Removal Unit) unveils groundbreaking technology. Powered by patented Molecular Magnetic Treatment, MARU eliminates diamagnetic arsenic ions without electricity or chemicals.

Learn More

M.A.R.U's Pilot Trials

MARU, Navmarg's arsenic removal technology, triumphed in pilot trials, treating 29 lakh liters of water across nine locations in two years. Ongoing trials at a Bihar school, serving 1135 children, demonstrate MARU's scalability. CSR Grant secured: 10.30 lakhs from ICICI Foundation for stability in high-scale deployments.

Learn More

Creating Awareness with M.A.R.U

Based on indigenous molecular magnetic arsenic repulsion technology, deployed for pilot testing since 2 years, in 5 school campus in bihar.MARU's pilot studies engaged schools, educating students and teachers on arsenic awareness, impacting 2300 children and 100+ households.MARU's societal impact and partnerships with organizations like UNICEF highlight its traction in addressing critical water quality issues.

Our Philanthropy

Through pilot trials and research grants, our societal contributions extended beyond technology.
