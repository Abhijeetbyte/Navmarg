Overview

METAL Molecular Magnetic Technology for Arsenic Removal

Our flagship solution, the Magnetic Arsenic Removal Unit, offers a sustainable and cost-effective approach to addressing arsenic contamination. Utilizing Molecular Magnetic Treatment, it ensures clean and safe drinking water at an incredibly low cost (Cost of Water per Litre: Nearly 0.1 Paise per litre).

With its highly scalable design, our unit is adaptable to any capacity, providing flexibility and ease of integration. Featuring advanced High Flow Rate Technology, it efficiently removes arsenic, achieving impressive results: @800 LPH - ~500ppb and @5000 LPH - ~2000ppb (as per recorded data).

  • Sustainability at Core: Sustainability is not just a goal; it's a guiding principle in everything we do. From concept to implementation, we prioritize eco-friendly practices and materials.
