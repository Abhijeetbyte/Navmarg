THE MAGNETIC ARSENIC REMOVAL UNIT

One of our flagship solutions is the Magnetic Arsenic Removal Unit (MARU), which utilizes Molecular Magnetic Treatment of Arsenic in a sustainable and cost effective manner (Cost of Water per Litre: Nearly 0.1 Paise per litre ) Highly scalable method suitable for any capacity, ensuring flexibility and design adaptability. High Flow Rate Technology for Arsenic Removal i.e., @800 LPH - ~500ppb, @5000 LPH - ~2000 (As per recorded during the Pilot Trial of Phase 2 and 1, 2021-2023).
