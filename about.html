WHO WE ARE

Navmarg Research and Innovations Pvt Ltd is pioneering water management solutions. We empower government agencies, CSR initiatives, and private R&D teams with comprehensive expertise. Our flagship Magnetic Arsenic Removal Unit (MARU) is cost-effective and sensor-equipped, ensuring top-notch water quality. Collaborations with top institutions and government partnerships drive our commitment to innovation in water sustainability.
